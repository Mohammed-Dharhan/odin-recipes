Cinnamon Rolls

Description

I'm going to show you how to make some healthy cinnamon rolls.... well not so much on the healthy side.

Ingredients

  1. Milk
  2. Sugar
  3. Quick rise yeast
  4. Butter
  5. Eggs
  6. Cinnamon, of course
  7. Salt

Steps

  1. Warm milk to around 110 degrees F.
  2. Place dough hook on stand mixer and knead dough on medium speed for 8 minutes.
  3. Transfer dough ball to a well-oiled bowl, cover with plastic wrap and a warm towel. Allow dough to rise for 1 hour to 1 ½ hours, or until doubled in size.
  4. After dough has doubled in size, transfer dough to a well-floured surface and roll out into a 14x9 inch rectangle. Spread softened butter over dough, leaving a ¼ inch margin at the far side of the dough.
  5. In a small bowl, mix together brown sugar and cinnamon.
  6. Tightly roll dough up, starting from the 9-inch side and place seam side down making sure to seal the edges of the dough as best you can.
  7. Cut into 1 inch sections with a serrated knife or floss.
  8. Place cinnamon rolls in a greased 9x9 inch baking pan or round 9 inch cake pan.
  9. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Remove plastic wrap and towel and bake cinnamon rolls for 20-25 minutes or until just slightly golden brown on the edges.
  10. Lastly.. Don't forget to enjoy
